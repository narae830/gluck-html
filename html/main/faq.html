How to use GLUCK

  • Q. How do I create an account?
    A. You can sign up with your email address or log- in with your Google account.
    To sign up with your email
    1. Select "Sign up" on the log-in page.
    2. Enter your email address and create a password and a nickname, then select Sign up.
    3. Open your email inbox to activate your account.
    4. Log-in on GLUCK.
    To sign up with Google
    1. Tap Google account.
    2. Enter your email address, password and a nickname, and then select Sign up
